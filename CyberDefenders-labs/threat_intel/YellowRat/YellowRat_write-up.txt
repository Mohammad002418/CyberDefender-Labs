# Lab Write-up: YellowRat

## Platform
CyberDefenders

## Category
Threat Intel
## Scenario:
Analyze malware artifacts using threat intelligence platforms like VirusTotal to identify
 IOCs, C2 servers, and understand adversary tactics.


## Tools Used
- VirusTotal.
- Red Canary.


## Analysis Steps
 1-Collect Artifacts

Gather malware files, hashes, or suspicious samples provided in the lab.

2-Upload to VirusTotal

3-Check file hash, see detections, and identify any malicious indicators (IOCs).

4-Check Threat Intelligence.

5-Use Red Canary or other TI platforms to see if the malware or related IOCs are known.

6-Identify C2 Servers.

7-Look at the domains, IPs, and URLs the malware contacts in reports.

8-Map Adversary Tactics

9-Match observed behavior (execution, persistence, communication) to MITRE ATT&CK techniques.

10-Document Findings.

Record IOCs, C2 info, and tactics for threat hunting or SOC referenc
